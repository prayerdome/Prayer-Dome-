Membership Requirements

  • Personal Faith: A personal confession of faith in Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior
  • Baptism: Water baptism by immersion (or willingness to be baptized)
  • Membership Class: Complete the Prayer Dome Membership Class
  • Statement of Faith: Agree with the Prayer Dome Statement of Faith
  • Regular Attendance: Commitment to regular attendance of services
  • Stewardship: Commitment to support the church with tithes and offerings
