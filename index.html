Our Divine Mandate

We exist to convict, equip, and release believers into their God-given purpose, grow faith, and make a difference through prayer, evangelism, and compassionate care. Rooted in Matthew 21:13, we are a global family transcending borders.

What We Believe

The doctrinal foundation of Prayer Dome — anchored in Scripture and the historic Christian faith.

Holy Scriptures

The Bible is inspired, infallible, and final authority (2 Tim 3:16-17).

OT/NTGod-breathed

Trinity & Jesus

One God in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Jesus Christ: fully God, virgin-born, sinless, crucified, risen, and returning.

Salvation by Grace

Justified by faith alone through Christ’s blood. Repentance and new birth are essential (Eph 2:8-9).

Water Baptism & Spirit

Believers' baptism by immersion; Baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence of gifts, empowering for witness.

Gifts & Fruit

Operation of spiritual gifts and development of the fruit of the Spirit in every believer (Gal 5:22-23).

Divine Healing

Healing provided through Christ’s atonement; we pray for the sick expecting miracles (James 5:14).

The Church & Fivefold Ministry

Body of Christ; apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors, teachers for equipping the saints (Eph 4:11-13).

Marriage & Family

Holy union between one man and one woman. Upholding Christian family values.

Second Coming & Eternity

Jesus will return bodily, resurrection of the dead, eternal life for the righteous.

Compassion Ministry Lord’s Supper (non-alcoholic) Prayer intercession Tithing & offerings

Spiritual Authority & Leadership

Apostolic governance — President, Pastors, Elders, and local assemblies submitted to Christ.

President's Committee

President + 6 Pastors. Oversees doctrine, discipline, and global coordination.

Local Assemblies

Pastor / Assembly Leader with local committee handles spiritual oversight, finances, and ministry.

Fivefold Ministry

Apostles, Prophets, Evangelists, Pastors, Teachers — serving the body.

"All officers must conform to 1 Timothy 3:1-13. The President holds authority to preach, baptize, and ordain. All matters of importance are submitted to the Presidents’ Committee."

Governing Constitution

Church Government

The Presidents’ Committee leads with final authority. Local Committees manage assemblies and report annually. Trustees oversee property and assets. Membership requires repentance, water baptism, and alignment with doctrine.

Ten percent of tithes to Central FundThree signatories for bank accounts

Discipline & Peace

Members and officers shall not cause division. Appeals go to the Presidents' Committee, whose ruling is final. Any leader leaving must not open a competing church within the same area.

"No alcohol for communion — grape juice only."

Global Branches

Prayer Dome operates in Eswatini (Headquarters: Manzini), Zambia (Chama), and Ireland (Cork). Each branch aligns with the central constitution.

info@prayerdome.net

Leadership Credentials

Ordained ministers receive credentials; certificates are subject to renewal. Pastors must be legally married to one spouse and uphold holiness. Retirement at age 60 (flexible extension).

Article of Faith: "The Prayer Dome is a fellowship where the Gospel is preached, widows and orphans are assisted (James 1:27), and the Holy Spirit's gifts operate in order."
