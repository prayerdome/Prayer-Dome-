The Power of Persistent Prayer
Luke 18:1 - "Then Jesus told his disciples a parable to show them that they should always pray and not give up."
Prayer is not just a religious activity; it is a lifeline to our Creator. Jesus taught us to pray persistently, not because God is reluctant to answer, but because persistent prayer changes us. It builds our faith, deepens our dependence on God, and aligns our will with His.
Prayer: Lord, teach me to pray without ceasing. Give me the persistence to keep bringing my requests before You, trusting that You hear and will answer according to Your perfect will. Amen.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Prayer Dome Ministry ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name and email address (when you register)
  • Phone number (optional, for contact purposes)
  • Profile photos (if you choose to upload)
  • Prayer requests and testimonies you choose to share
  • Church membership information (if you apply)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data and interactions
  • IP address and approximate location (country level only)
  • Notification preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service - including user authentication, prayer wall, and community features
  • To communicate with you - about events, prayer requests, and updates you've requested
  • To improve our app - analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • To process membership applications - if you choose to apply for church membership
  • To send notifications - only with your explicit permission

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Public prayers and testimonies: When you choose to make them public, they are visible to other users
  • Service providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and storage. Their privacy policy applies to data handled by their services
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With your consent: Any other sharing with your explicit permission

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers located in the United States and Europe
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure

6. Children's Privacy

Prayer Dome is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information (via Edit Profile)
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of notifications at any time
  • Export your data (contact us for assistance)

8. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal purposes.

9. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to external sites (Facebook, WhatsApp, etc.). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Information

For privacy concerns or data requests:

Email: privacy@prayerdome.net
Phone: +268 7658 1804
Address: Prayer Dome Ministry, Ngculwini, Eswatini

12. Data Deletion Request

To request deletion of your account and all associated data:

  1. Email us at privacy@prayerdome.net from your registered email
  2. Include the subject line "Delete My Account"
  3. We will process your request within 14 days
  4. You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete

Note: Public prayers and testimonies may remain anonymized after account deletion.
